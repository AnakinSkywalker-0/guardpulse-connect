DIGITAL PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION ACT 2023 - INTERNAL COMPLIANCE POLICY

1. Data Fiduciary Responsibilities
Every Data Fiduciary shall ensure that personal data is processed only for the specific purpose for which the Data Principal has given consent. 

2. Rights of Data Principals
Data Principals have the right to access information about their personal data, the right to correction, and the right to grievance redressal.

3. Breach Notification
In the event of a personal data breach, the Data Fiduciary must notify the Data Protection Board of India and each affected Data Principal immediately.
