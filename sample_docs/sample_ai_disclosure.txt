AI SYSTEM DISCLOSURE DOCUMENT — TechFlow Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
Product: TechFlow Predict (AI-Powered Business Forecasting Engine)
Version: 2.1
Date: January 2025

1. SYSTEM DESCRIPTION
TechFlow Predict is an AI-powered forecasting tool that analyses historical
business data to generate revenue predictions, customer churn scores, and
inventory recommendations for enterprise clients.

2. AI TECHNIQUES USED
- Gradient Boosting (XGBoost) for tabular prediction
- LSTM neural networks for time-series forecasting
- Large Language Model (GPT-4 based) for natural language report generation
- Automated decision-making for low-risk inventory reorder suggestions

3. RISK CLASSIFICATION
We consider TechFlow Predict to be a limited-risk AI system.
The system does not make final decisions — all outputs are recommendations
reviewed by human analysts before action is taken.

4. INTENDED USE
- Revenue forecasting for finance teams
- Customer churn prediction for CRM teams
- Inventory optimisation for supply chain managers
The system is not intended for use in healthcare, law enforcement,
credit scoring, or recruitment decisions.

5. TRANSPARENCY TO USERS
Users are informed at login that AI-generated recommendations are present.
All AI outputs are labelled with a confidence score (0-100%).
Users can request human review of any AI recommendation.

6. DATA USED FOR TRAINING
Training data consists of anonymised enterprise transaction records.
No personal data of end consumers is used in model training.
Data governance review was conducted in Q3 2024.

7. HUMAN OVERSIGHT
A human analyst must approve any recommendation exceeding $50,000 in value.
The system includes an override mechanism for all automated suggestions.
Monthly audits are performed by our AI Safety team.

8. PERFORMANCE AND ACCURACY
Average forecast accuracy: 87% (MAPE < 13%)
The system has known limitations with data from newly formed companies
(less than 12 months of history).

9. PROHIBITED USES
This system must NOT be used for:
- Profiling individuals based on protected characteristics
- Predicting individual employee performance
- Any use case involving biometric data
- Real-time remote biometric identification

10. INCIDENT REPORTING
AI-related incidents must be reported to ai-safety@techflow.io within 72 hours.
Serious incidents are escalated to our board within 24 hours.
