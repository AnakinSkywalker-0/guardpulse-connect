PRIVACY POLICY — TechFlow Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
Last Updated: January 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
TechFlow Analytics Pvt. Ltd. ("Company", "we", "us") operates a B2B SaaS platform
for business intelligence and data analytics. This Privacy Policy explains how we
collect, use, store, and share personal data when you use our services.

2. DATA WE COLLECT
We collect the following categories of personal data:
- Full name and email address of registered users
- Phone number and business address
- Payment and billing information including credit card details
- IP addresses and device identifiers
- Browser cookies and session tokens
- Usage logs and browsing history within the platform
- Aadhaar number (for KYC verification of Indian enterprise customers)
- Date of birth (for account verification)

3. PURPOSE OF COLLECTION
We collect personal data for the following purposes:
- To provide and improve our analytics services
- To process payments and manage billing
- For customer support and communication
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- For internal analytics and product improvement

4. DATA SHARING
We may share your personal data with:
- Third-party payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe)
- Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
- Analytics partners for aggregate reporting
- Law enforcement when required by court order

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. DATA RETENTION
We retain personal data for as long as your account is active.
Upon account deletion, data is removed within 90 days from our primary systems.
Backup copies may be retained for up to 12 months.

6. USER RIGHTS
You may contact us to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account data

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@techflow.io

7. CHILDREN'S DATA
Our platform is intended for business use only (18+).
We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. SECURITY
We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest.
All data in transit is protected by TLS 1.3.
We conduct annual security audits.
